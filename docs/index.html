About Us

R. P. Palija & Co, Chartered Accountants, was established in 1973 by CA Ranjit Paleja. Prior to setting up of the firm he was part of A.F. Ferguson & Associates (now a member firm of PWC) for 5 years.

We believe in rendering services of the highest professional standards and carry the legacy of vast experience and varied exposure in the industry. Our creditable records streamlined with up-to-date practicing tools and expertise help us deliver value-added services.

We offer client specific value added services in the field of Taxation, Assurance, Business Advisory and Corporate Laws. Our professional approach blended with personal touch has earned us enormous confidence of all our clients, which is reflected in the enduring business relationships that we enjoy with them, as also in the consistent growth in the portfolio of our services.

We are committed to meeting the expectations of our clients by maintaining international standards and fully exploiting the advantages of technology.
